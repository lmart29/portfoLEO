Reconnecting with the Earth
January 2025

This sculpture came out of a very long “passion hiatus”.
Depression had me in a chokehold, but as time went on,
its grip loosened on my neck. Before RWTE, I made art
not for the joy of making it, but to pass the class.
With this sculpture, I was finally free to enjoy who I
am and enjoy art. There is no special meaning; there is
no secret symbolism; there is no nothing. Just here for
our viewing pleasure.
